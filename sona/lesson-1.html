About Toki Pona

Toki Pona is a constructed language created by Sonja Lang in 2001. It is known for its simplicity, consisting of around 120 words and a straightforward grammar structure.

Explore Toki Pona and improve your language skills through our resources:

  • sona: Dive into Toki Pona lessons to learn vocabulary and grammar.
  • kulupu nimi: Access a comprehensive Toki Pona dictionary.
  • ijo pona: Enjoy interactive games for practicing Toki Pona.
